Rules

To start a game, there must be atleast two players. When two playernames has been entered, the timer will start a countdown from a random time between 1 and 6 minutes. When the timer hits 00:00.000, a name will popup on the middle of the screen, a voice will yell out his/hers name, and the screen will go crazy with blinking and sounds.

If you're the one that is picked out to drink, depending on the local rules on the game, you have to drink! (You should figure out how much before starting the game) A suggestion on how to make the game more fun, is to get a cardboard box and have the drinkee stand in it while drinking.

There are two modes to the game. Anarchy, where a name is picked random, always, and fair. Fair works like this: A player can never be more than 3 drinks ahead than the other players. This means the namepicking is pseudorandom up untill someone has drunk 3 times, then that person isn't accounted for anymore, untill everyone else has drunk 3 times.
